# Software Requirements Specification

## 1. Introduction

## 1.1. Purpose

The purpose of orate-io is to provide a platform for AI-powered analysis of speeches. The analytics include information relating to grammar, speech vocabulary, tone and intonation, body language, eye contact, pace, and filler word use. The motivation behind the analytics of orate-io is to evaluate speeches and give metrics feedback to track and further improve the user's speaking skills.

## 1.2. Intended Use

The product is intended to be used by any user who wishes to improve their speaking skills. It may be used by organizations or individuals for training or for recieving feedback on speeches.

## 1.3. Scope

The project will provide to be a useful tool for improving public speaking. An organization with a similar goal is Tooastmasters, which provides speech feedback. This feedback, just like orate-io relates to filler word count, eye contat, delivery, and speech grammar and content. Orate-io provides a free, open platform to perform these analyses for anyone who is unassociated with the Toastmasters organization.
